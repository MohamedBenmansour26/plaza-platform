Create a fully clickable mobile prototype (375px) for Plaza — 
the delivery driver app. Drivers are Moroccan delivery agents 
who receive orders, pick them up from merchants, and deliver 
to customers. All text in French. Use Inter font.

This is a mobile-only app (375px). No desktop version needed.
Drivers use their phone exclusively.

Design system:
  Primary: #2563EB | Accent: #E8632A | Success: #16A34A
  Error: #DC2626 | Warning: #D97706 | Dark: #1C1917
  Mid: #78716C | Surface: #FAFAF9 | Card: #FFFFFF
  Radius: 8px standard, 12px cards, 9999px badges
  Spacing: 8px grid | Font: Inter

Navigation: bottom tab bar (3 tabs, always visible):
  Livraisons (truck icon) | Historique (clock icon) | Profil (user icon)
  Active: #2563EB icon + label. Inactive: #78716C.
